Codeforces 975 D. Valhalla Siege

发表于 2018-05-17 | 分类于 计划 |

英文题面

翻译

  给定直线$y = ax + b$上的若干个点的x,以及这些点的横向速度以及纵向速度. 求这些点会相交多少次.

提示

  两个点相遇的条件是什么?

代码

分析

  两个点相遇的条件是：在同一时刻，它们的横坐标相同，纵坐标相同，那么我们可以针对横坐标和纵坐标的时间列一个方程，解得：$v_{ay} - av_{ax} = v_{by} - av_{bx}$. 利用map存$v_y - av_x$的值有多少个，每次在map中查找个数即可.
  因为列的方程的分母可能为0，需要再建一个map存所有的直线(由横速度与纵速度构成)，来保证不计数平行的直线.

总结

  对问题的条件做进一步的推导与转化. 若要计数同类元素，记录值域信息！

算法竞赛进阶指南第五章

发表于 2018-05-16 | 分类于 算法竞赛进阶指南 |

前言

  历时3天时间完成了这一章的学习. 有一些数据结构有很好的应用.

1.并查集

  无向图连通情况，传递关系，位置占用(连成一片),带权并查集，扩展域并查集. 当位置连成一片可以跳过时，可以利用并查集维护每个点往右边跳能跳到哪个点.(跳过一整段连续的区域).
  几个经典的用并查集维护的关系：
  1.对立集合.
  2.同类集合.

2.树状数组

  树状数组区间修改+区间查询,思想是将整体求和转化为统计单点贡献.

3.线段树

  线段树维护一段数的gcd,利用定理$gcd(x,y) = gcd(x,y - x)$,维护序列首位元素以及差分数组.

4.扫描线

  将无法“准确”计算的矩形变成对称的线段. 因为对称，所以不用pushdown,pushdown是为了处理查询的区间是修改区间的子区间这种情况.,维护区间内的点是否被完全覆盖. 一个长度为2的区间不可再分，而在线段树中可以，故要将右端点-1.
  求一类有限制的极值问题，要搞清楚到底是什么产生了极值，针对它设计算法,对限制进行转化.比如选点连通问题、机器任务问题、矩形星星问题等等.

5.分块

  分块的常用方法:
  1.整体排序，块内排序.
  2.块内二分求值.
  3.对变量大小进行分类讨论，采取不同的方法. 比如若变量小，数组能开的下，就维护块的前缀和. 否则暴力统计.
  4.解决二维偏序的“which”问题.

6.一类从后往前依次确定的题.

  poj2828,poj2182. 最后一个数可以立马确定. 并且前面的数可以通过后面的数以及自身的值来确定.

poj3580 SuperMemo

发表于 2018-05-16 | 分类于 算法竞赛进阶指南 |

英文题面

Description

Your friend, Jackson is invited to a TV show called SuperMemo in which the participant is told to play a memorizing game. At first, the host tells the participant a sequence of numbers, {A1, A2, … An}. Then the host performs a series of operations and queries on the sequence which consists:

ADD x y D: Add D to each number in sub-sequence {Ax … Ay}. For example, performing “ADD 2 4 1” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} results in {1, 3, 4, 5, 5}
REVERSE x y: reverse the sub-sequence {Ax … Ay}. For example, performing “REVERSE 2 4” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} results in {1, 4, 3, 2, 5}
REVOLVE x y T: rotate sub-sequence {Ax … Ay} T times. For example, performing “REVOLVE 2 4 2” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} results in {1, 3, 4, 2, 5}
INSERT x P: insert P after Ax. For example, performing “INSERT 2 4” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} results in {1, 2, 4, 3, 4, 5}
DELETE x: delete Ax. For example, performing “DELETE 2” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} results in {1, 3, 4, 5}
MIN x y: query the participant what is the minimum number in sub-sequence {Ax … Ay}. For example, the correct answer to “MIN 2 4” on {1, 2, 3, 4, 5} is 2
To make the show more interesting, the participant is granted a chance to turn to someone else that means when Jackson feels difficult in answering a query he may call you for help. You task is to watch the TV show and write a program giving the correct answer to each query in order to assist Jackson whenever he calls.

Input

The first line contains n (n ≤ 100000).

The following n lines describe the sequence.

Then follows M (M ≤ 100000), the numbers of operations and queries.

The following M lines describe the operations and queries.

Output

For each “MIN” query, output the correct answer.

Sample Input

5
1
2
3
4
5
2
ADD 2 4 1
MIN 4 5
Sample Output

5

POJ Founder Monthly Contest – 2008.04.13, Yao Jinyu

代码

分析

Codeforces 979 D.Kuro and GCD and XOR and SUM

发表于 2018-05-15 | 分类于 计划 |

英文题面

翻译

两种操作，要么往集合中插入一个数，要么给定x,k,s,求集合中的一个v,使得$k | gcd(x,v),x + v ≤ s$并且x异或v的值要求最大.

提示

  两个数异或最大能想到什么？

代码

分析

  由两个数异或得到的值最大可以想到Trie树(不要想线性基，那是多个数异或得到的值最大). 关键是限制不好处理. 如何才能保证gcd被k整除？与x的和≤s?
  对于第二个限制，我们可以在Trie树上记录当前节点子树叶节点的最小值，若要往下一层走，则先判断最小值+x是否≤s. 是则可以往下走. 对于第一个限制，要使得x与v的gcd被k整除，那么x,v一定都要是k的倍数. 先判断x是不是k的倍数，若不是，直接返回-1. v也要是k的倍数，在一棵Trie树上不好处理，因为在没有走到叶子节点以前不能判断这个数的具体组成. 那么就用10w棵Trie树,第i棵Trie树存的是被i整除的数,每次询问只需要在第k棵Trie树上查询即可.
  事实上这道题还可以用比较巧妙的暴力过掉：对k进行分类处理. 若$k > 100$,则直接找k的倍数，看它与x的异或值，更新答案. 若$k < 100$,则从s到1枚举异或得到的答案，看v是否满足答案. 跑的比std还快，不科学..

总结

  难点是对限制的处理. 不等式限制可以用极值法解决，而倍数关系则要对数分类. 要想到这个数在以后的哪些查询中可能会被考虑到.

poj1417 True Liars

发表于 2018-05-13 | 分类于 算法竞赛进阶指南 |

英文题面

Description

After having drifted about in a small boat for a couple of days, Akira Crusoe Maeda was finally cast ashore on a foggy island. Though he was exhausted and despaired, he was still fortunate to remember a legend of the foggy island, which he had heard from patriarchs in his childhood. This must be the island in the legend. In the legend, two tribes have inhabited the island, one is divine and the other is devilish, once members of the divine tribe bless you, your future is bright and promising, and your soul will eventually go to Heaven, in contrast, once members of the devilish tribe curse you, your future is bleak and hopeless, and your soul will eventually fall down to Hell.

In order to prevent the worst-case scenario, Akira should distinguish the devilish from the divine. But how? They looked exactly alike and he could not distinguish one from the other solely by their appearances. He still had his last hope, however. The members of the divine tribe are truth-tellers, that is, they always tell the truth and those of the devilish tribe are liars, that is, they always tell a lie.

He asked some of them whether or not some are divine. They knew one another very much and always responded to him “faithfully” according to their individual natures (i.e., they always tell the truth or always a lie). He did not dare to ask any other forms of questions, since the legend says that a devilish member would curse a person forever when he did not like the question. He had another piece of useful informationf the legend tells the populations of both tribes. These numbers in the legend are trustworthy since everyone living on this island is immortal and none have ever been born at least these millennia.

You are a good computer programmer and so requested to help Akira by writing a program that classifies the inhabitants according to their answers to his inquiries.
Input

The input consists of multiple data sets, each in the following format :

n p1 p2
xl yl a1
x2 y2 a2
…
xi yi ai
…
xn yn an

The first line has three non-negative integers n, p1, and p2. n is the number of questions Akira asked. pl and p2 are the populations of the divine and devilish tribes, respectively, in the legend. Each of the following n lines has two integers xi, yi and one word ai. xi and yi are the identification numbers of inhabitants, each of which is between 1 and p1 + p2, inclusive. ai is either yes, if the inhabitant xi said that the inhabitant yi was a member of the divine tribe, or no, otherwise. Note that xi and yi can be the same number since “are you a member of the divine tribe?” is a valid question. Note also that two lines may have the same x’s and y’s since Akira was very upset and might have asked the same question to the same one more than once.

You may assume that n is less than 1000 and that p1 and p2 are less than 300. A line with three zeros, i.e., 0 0 0, represents the end of the input. You can assume that each data set is consistent and no contradictory answers are included.

Output

For each data set, if it includes sufficient information to classify all the inhabitants, print the identification numbers of all the divine ones in ascending order, one in a line. In addition, following the output numbers, print end in a line. Otherwise, i.e., if a given data set does not include sufficient information to identify all the divine members, print no in a line.
Sample Input

2 1 1
1 2 no
2 1 no
3 2 1
1 1 yes
2 2 yes
3 3 yes
2 2 1
1 2 yes
2 3 no
5 4 3
1 2 yes
1 3 no
4 5 yes
5 6 yes
6 7 no
0 0 0
Sample Output

no
no
1
2
end
3
4
5
6
end
Source

Japan 2002 Kanazawa

翻译

有p1个好人,p2个坏人，现在给出m个关系，诸如a说b是好人，a说b是坏人. 好人一定说真话，坏人一定说假话，问能否给出唯一的方案满足上述限制？

分析

  用图论的观点去思考.

代码

分析

  如果有一条关系是“a说b yes or no”,那么就在a,b之间连一条边. 这样就会形成一个有多个连通分量的图. 对于每个连通分量，一定存在一个入度为0的点，确定这个人是好人or坏人，那么这个连通分量就会有两种结果. 记这个人为好人时，该连通分量的好人数为$cnt_1$,为坏人是，好人数为$cnt_2$. 因为每个连通分量都是独立的，问题就转变成了一个背包问题：将每个连通分量看作一个物品，取(cnt1)or不取(cnt2),令$f_{i,j}$表示前i个连通分量中，好人数量为j的方案数. 若最后$f_{cnt,p1}$不为1，则输出no.
  输出方案怎么解决？从后往前枚举连通分量. 看这个状态是从哪个状态转移而来的. 因为最后$f_{cnt,p1}$为1，要么$f_{cnt - 1,p1 - cnt_{0,i}}$为1，要么$f_{cnt - 1,p1 - cnt_{1,i}}$为1，确定了转移方向后，更新p1,将符合要求的点加入答案中，最后排序输出即可.
  可以利用带权并查集来处理连通分量. 因为yes表示a与b同类,no表示a与b不是同类，维护每个点是不是与连通分量的根的同类即可.

经验教训

  模型见少了，就差最后一步的背包转化.

bzoj2821 作诗

发表于 2018-05-13 | 分类于 算法竞赛进阶指南 |

中文题面

Description
神犇SJY虐完HEOI之后给傻×LYD出了一题：SHY是T国的公主，平时的一大爱好是作诗。由于时间紧迫，SHY作完诗
之后还要虐OI，于是SHY找来一篇长度为N的文章，阅读M次，每次只阅读其中连续的一段[l,r]，从这一段中选出一
些汉字构成诗。因为SHY喜欢对偶，所以SHY规定最后选出的每个汉字都必须在[l,r]里出现了正偶数次。而且SHY认
为选出的汉字的种类数（两个一样的汉字称为同一种）越多越好（为了拿到更多的素材！）。于是SHY请LYD安排选
法。LYD这种傻×当然不会了，于是向你请教……问题简述：N个数，M组询问，每次问[l,r]中有多少个数出现正偶
数次。
Input
输入第一行三个整数n、c以及m。表示文章字数、汉字的种类数、要选择M次。第二行有n个整数，每个数Ai在[1, c
]间，代表一个编码为Ai的汉字。接下来m行每行两个整数l和r，设上一个询问的答案为ans(第一个询问时ans=0)，
令L=(l+ans)mod n+1, R=(r+ans)mod n+1，若L>R，交换L和R，则本次询问为[L,R]。
Output
输出共m行，每行一个整数，第i个数表示SHY第i次能选出的汉字的最多种类数。

Sample Input
5 3 5

1 2 2 3 1

0 4

1 2

2 2

2 3

3 5
Sample Output
2

0

0

0

1
HINT
对于100%的数据，1<=n,c,m<=10^5
Source
By lydrainbowcat

代码

分析

  如果做过蒲公英那道题，很容易就能想到这道题可以用分块来做. 记录一个数组$f_{i,j}$表示第i个块到第j个块内出现次数为偶数的数有多少个. 可以通过$O(sqrt(n)n)$预处理得到.然后用一个vector存储每个数值在哪些位置出现了.
  对于查询操作，先查整块内的值：若l属于块p,r属于块q,$ans = f_{p + 1,q - 1}$,然后暴力统计不在整块内的数在给定区间内是否出现了偶数次，利用vector对区间的左右端点进行lower_bound和upper_bound运算. 为了避免重复统计，可以利用vis数组标记已经统计过的值，因为值域很小，不需要离散化. 查询操作很多，利用时间戳来替代vis的清空. 这样做会出现一个问题：可能有的数在整块内出现了偶数次，在区间[l,r]内出现了奇数次，那么这个数对应的答案就要减掉;或者在整块内出现了奇数次，在[l,r]内出现了偶数次，这个数对应的答案就要加上. 对这两种情况进行特殊判断即可.
  需要注意的是题目要求的出现偶数次是正偶数次，统计的时候特判出现0次的情况!

poj2912 Rochambeau

发表于 2018-05-13 | 分类于 算法竞赛进阶指南 |

英文题面

Description

N children are playing Rochambeau (scissors-rock-cloth) game with you. One of them is the judge. The rest children are divided into three groups (it is possible that some group is empty). You don’t know who is the judge, or how the children are grouped. Then the children start playing Rochambeau game for M rounds. Each round two children are arbitrarily selected to play Rochambeau for one once, and you will be told the outcome while not knowing which gesture the children presented. It is known that the children in the same group would present the same gesture (hence, two children in the same group always get draw when playing) and different groups for different gestures. The judge would present gesture randomly each time, hence no one knows what gesture the judge would present. Can you guess who is the judge after after the game ends? If you can, after how many rounds can you find out the judge at the earliest?

Input

Input contains multiple test cases. Each test case starts with two integers N and M (1 ≤ N ≤ 500, 0 ≤ M ≤ 2,000) in one line, which are the number of children and the number of rounds. Following are M lines, each line contains two integers in [0, N) separated by one symbol. The two integers are the IDs of the two children selected to play Rochambeau for this round. The symbol may be “=”, “>” or “<”, referring to a draw, that first child wins and that second child wins respectively.

Output

There is only one line for each test case. If the judge can be found, print the ID of the judge, and the least number of rounds after which the judge can be uniquely determined. If the judge can not be found, or the outcomes of the M rounds of game are inconsistent, print the corresponding message.

Sample Input

3 3
0<1 0="" 3="" 5="" 1<2="" 2<0="" 0<1="">1
1<2 1="">2
0<2 0="" 4="" 0<1="">1
2<3 2="">3
1 0
Sample Output

Can not determine
Player 1 can be determined to be the judge after 4 lines
Impossible
Player 0 can be determined to be the judge after 0 lines
Source

Baidu Star 2006 Preliminary
Chen, Shixi (xreborner) living in http://fairyair.yeah.net/

翻译

  有3个集合，有n-1个人属于这3个集合中的1个，它们玩石头剪刀布游戏，在每个集合的人会固定出. 有一个裁判，会随机出，现在给出m轮胜负情况，求出裁判是谁，并求出是在第几轮后能判断出裁判是谁的.

代码

分析

  判断裁判是谁的依据是出现矛盾的局面, 我一开始的想法是枚举裁判i，若i存在的关系中出现了第二次矛盾，那么i就可能是裁判，并且第二次出现矛盾的局数就是判断i的局数. 为什么是第二次出现矛盾呢？ 比如1 < 0,0 < 1,出现了一次矛盾，但是不能知道裁判是谁，若1 < 2,1 > 2,就能判断裁判是1,此时枚举的是1. 利用并查集来维护. 事实上这种做法不行，理论上可行，然而实现上有问题. 因为一旦处理到第一次出现矛盾，就要将集合还原,并查集不支持分离操作,若每次重新开一个数组记录原来的fa信息以便还原，会因为复杂度太高而T掉.
  正确的做法是枚举裁判i,并忽视i出现的关系. 若没有出现矛盾，说明i可能是裁判. 因为只有裁判才可能使得局面出现矛盾. 正好与我的做法反了过来. 如果最后能成为裁判的人数≥2,则输出can not determine,若没有人能成为裁判，则输出impossible. 那么如何求在第几轮判断出裁判是谁呢？枚举裁判为i的过程中，若出现矛盾，记录下第一次出现矛盾的轮数$error_i$,取最大值即为答案.为什么要这么求?还是上面那种情况,1<0,0<1,我们把i当作裁判，不考虑i出现的关系，实际上就是排除i当裁判的可能,这样处理得到的答案就是第二次出现矛盾的局数.

反思

  没能推出来主要还是思想上的问题.总是想着有它一定存在矛盾，不能想到没他一定没有矛盾. 并查集不支持分离操作.
