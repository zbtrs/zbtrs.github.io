###分析   因为n只有5000,我们可以把$f_{l,r}$预处理出来，然后做一次dp,O(1)可完成查询操作，难点就在于怎么预处理.   观察f函数，它是由相邻的两项异或而组成一个新的序列. 联想到杨辉三角，将$f_{l,r}$看作杨辉三角的最后一行，往上递推，一定能递推到一行只剩一个数. 预处理f函数的关键在于找到规律. 没有直接能表达出$f_{l,r}$的式子，就要找递推式. f函数构成的杨辉三角的每一个数是由它下面的数和它下面的右边的数异或得到的. 那么可以得到：$f_{l,r} = f_{l + 1,r} ^ f_{l,r - 1}$. 用记忆化搜索，即可$O(n^2)$预处理得到所有的f值. 接下来的dp就很容易了. ###经验   一个函数是递归定义的，并且与每一对相邻的数有关，那么可以联想到杨辉三角.

Codeforces 984 C. Finite or not?

发表于 2018-05-17 | 分类于 计划

###英文题面 ###翻译 问p/q在b进制下是不是有限小数. ###提示   先考虑十进制. ###代码

###分析   一个结论：在十进制下，若p/q化成最简分数后，q的质因子只有2和5，那么p/q就是一个有限小数，否则就是无限小数.   证明：在十进制下，一个有限小数一定可以表示成$p/10^k$的形式,而$10^k$只含有2和5两种质因子，故q要能变成$10^k$,就必须只能含有2和5. 同样的，对于b进制下的p/q,若最简分数q的质因子在b中都出现过，那么p/q就可以表示成一个有限小数.   怎么知道q的质因子在b中是否都出现过呢？ 求出它的所有质因子？复杂度太高了！可以利用唯一分解定律和gcd. 因为$gcd(a,b) = p_1^{min(a_1,b_1)}p_2^{min(a_2,b_2)}......p_k^{min(a_k,b_k)}$. 求q与b的gcd,并将q除去它. 一直除下去，知道gcd = 1,那么q中含有的b的质因数一定都被除完了，剩下的就是b没有的质因数，只需要判断q是否等于1即可.

###英文题面 ###翻译   给定直线$y = ax + b$上的若干个点的x,以及这些点的横向速度以及纵向速度. 求这些点会相交多少次. ###提示   两个点相遇的条件是什么? ###代码

###分析   两个点相遇的条件是：在同一时刻，它们的横坐标相同，纵坐标相同，那么我们可以针对横坐标和纵坐标的时间列一个方程，解得：$v_{ay} - av_{ax} = v_{by} - av_{bx}$. 利用map存$v_y - av_x$的值有多少个，每次在map中查找个数即可.   因为列的方程的分母可能为0，需要再建一个map存所有的直线(由横速度与纵速度构成)，来保证不计数平行的直线. ###总结   对问题的条件做进一步的推导与转化. 若要计数同类元素，记录值域信息！

###前言   历时3天时间完成了这一章的学习. 有一些数据结构有很好的应用. ###1.并查集   无向图连通情况，传递关系，位置占用(连成一片),带权并查集，扩展域并查集. 当位置连成一片可以跳过时，可以利用并查集维护每个点往右边跳能跳到哪个点.(跳过一整段连续的区域).   几个经典的用并查集维护的关系：   1.对立集合.   2.同类集合. ###2.树状数组   树状数组区间修改+区间查询,思想是将整体求和转化为统计单点贡献. ###3.线段树   线段树维护一段数的gcd,利用定理$gcd(x,y) = gcd(x,y - x)$,维护序列首位元素以及差分数组. ###4.扫描线   将无法“准确”计算的矩形变成对称的线段. 因为对称，所以不用pushdown,pushdown是为了处理查询的区间是修改区间的子区间这种情况.,维护区间内的点是否被完全覆盖. 一个长度为2的区间不可再分，而在线段树中可以，故要将右端点-1.   求一类有限制的极值问题，要搞清楚到底是什么产生了极值，针对它设计算法,对限制进行转化.比如选点连通问题、机器任务问题、矩形星星问题等等. ###5.分块   分块的常用方法:   1.整体排序，块内排序.   2.块内二分求值.   3.对变量大小进行分类讨论，采取不同的方法. 比如若变量小，数组能开的下，就维护块的前缀和. 否则暴力统计.   4.解决二维偏序的“which”问题. ###6.一类从后往前依次确定的题.   poj2828,poj2182. 最后一个数可以立马确定. 并且前面的数可以通过后面的数以及自身的值来确定.

###英文题面 ###翻译 两种操作，要么往集合中插入一个数，要么给定x,k,s,求集合中的一个v,使得$k | gcd(x,v),x + v ≤ s$并且x异或v的值要求最大. ###提示   两个数异或最大能想到什么？ ###代码

###分析   由两个数异或得到的值最大可以想到Trie树(不要想线性基，那是多个数异或得到的值最大). 关键是限制不好处理. 如何才能保证gcd被k整除？与x的和≤s?   对于第二个限制，我们可以在Trie树上记录当前节点子树叶节点的最小值，若要往下一层走，则先判断最小值+x是否≤s. 是则可以往下走. 对于第一个限制，要使得x与v的gcd被k整除，那么x,v一定都要是k的倍数. 先判断x是不是k的倍数，若不是，直接返回-1. v也要是k的倍数，在一棵Trie树上不好处理，因为在没有走到叶子节点以前不能判断这个数的具体组成. 那么就用10w棵Trie树,第i棵Trie树存的是被i整除的数,每次询问只需要在第k棵Trie树上查询即可.   事实上这道题还可以用比较巧妙的暴力过掉：对k进行分类处理. 若$k > 100$,则直接找k的倍数，看它与x的异或值，更新答案. 若$k < 100$,则从s到1枚举异或得到的答案，看v是否满足答案. 跑的比std还快，不科学.. ###总结   难点是对限制的处理. 不等式限制可以用极值法解决，而倍数关系则要对数分类. 要想到这个数在以后的哪些查询中可能会被考虑到.

poj1417 True Liars

###英文题面 Description

After having drifted about in a small boat for a couple of days, Akira Crusoe Maeda was finally cast ashore on a foggy island. Though he was exhausted and despaired, he was still fortunate to remember a legend of the foggy island, which he had heard from patriarchs in his childhood. This must be the island in the legend. In the legend, two tribes have inhabited the island, one is divine and the other is devilish, once members of the divine tribe bless you, your future is bright and promising, and your soul will eventually go to Heaven, in contrast, once members of the devilish tribe curse you, your future is bleak and hopeless, and your soul will eventually fall down to Hell.

In order to prevent the worst-case scenario, Akira should distinguish the devilish from the divine. But how? They looked exactly alike and he could not distinguish one from the other solely by their appearances. He still had his last hope, however. The members of the divine tribe are truth-tellers, that is, they always tell the truth and those of the devilish tribe are liars, that is, they always tell a lie.

He asked some of them whether or not some are divine. They knew one another very much and always responded to him "faithfully" according to their individual natures (i.e., they always tell the truth or always a lie). He did not dare to ask any other forms of questions, since the legend says that a devilish member would curse a person forever when he did not like the question. He had another piece of useful informationf the legend tells the populations of both tribes. These numbers in the legend are trustworthy since everyone living on this island is immortal and none have ever been born at least these millennia.

You are a good computer programmer and so requested to help Akira by writing a program that classifies the inhabitants according to their answers to his inquiries. Input

The input consists of multiple data sets, each in the following format :

n p1 p2 xl yl a1 x2 y2 a2 ... xi yi ai ... xn yn an

The first line has three non-negative integers n, p1, and p2. n is the number of questions Akira asked. pl and p2 are the populations of the divine and devilish tribes, respectively, in the legend. Each of the following n lines has two integers xi, yi and one word ai. xi and yi are the identification numbers of inhabitants, each of which is between 1 and p1 + p2, inclusive. ai is either yes, if the inhabitant xi said that the inhabitant yi was a member of the divine tribe, or no, otherwise. Note that xi and yi can be the same number since "are you a member of the divine tribe?" is a valid question. Note also that two lines may have the same x's and y's since Akira was very upset and might have asked the same question to the same one more than once.

You may assume that n is less than 1000 and that p1 and p2 are less than 300. A line with three zeros, i.e., 0 0 0, represents the end of the input. You can assume that each data set is consistent and no contradictory answers are included.

Output

For each data set, if it includes sufficient information to classify all the inhabitants, print the identification numbers of all the divine ones in ascending order, one in a line. In addition, following the output numbers, print end in a line. Otherwise, i.e., if a given data set does not include sufficient information to identify all the divine members, print no in a line. Sample Input

2 1 1 1 2 no 2 1 no 3 2 1 1 1 yes 2 2 yes 3 3 yes 2 2 1 1 2 yes 2 3 no 5 4 3 1 2 yes 1 3 no 4 5 yes 5 6 yes 6 7 no 0 0 0 Sample Output

no no 1 2 end 3 4 5 6 end Source

Japan 2002 Kanazawa ###翻译 有p1个好人,p2个坏人，现在给出m个关系，诸如a说b是好人，a说b是坏人. 好人一定说真话，坏人一定说假话，问能否给出唯一的方案满足上述限制？ ###分析   用图论的观点去思考. ###代码

###分析   如果有一条关系是“a说b yes or no",那么就在a,b之间连一条边. 这样就会形成一个有多个连通分量的图. 对于每个连通分量，一定存在一个入度为0的点，确定这个人是好人or坏人，那么这个连通分量就会有两种结果. 记这个人为好人时，该连通分量的好人数为$cnt_1$,为坏人是，好人数为$cnt_2$. 因为每个连通分量都是独立的，问题就转变成了一个背包问题：将每个连通分量看作一个物品，取(cnt1)or不取(cnt2),令$f_{i,j}$表示前i个连通分量中，好人数量为j的方案数. 若最后$f_{cnt,p1}$不为1，则输出no.   输出方案怎么解决？从后往前枚举连通分量. 看这个状态是从哪个状态转移而来的. 因为最后$f_{cnt,p1}$为1，要么$f_{cnt - 1,p1 - cnt_{0,i}}$为1，要么$f_{cnt - 1,p1 - cnt_{1,i}}$为1，确定了转移方向后，更新p1,将符合要求的点加入答案中，最后排序输出即可.   可以利用带权并查集来处理连通分量. 因为yes表示a与b同类,no表示a与b不是同类，维护每个点是不是与连通分量的根的同类即可. ###经验教训   模型见少了，就差最后一步的背包转化.
